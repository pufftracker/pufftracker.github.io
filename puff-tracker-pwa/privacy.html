Privacy Policy

At Puff Tracker, we are deeply committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and safeguarding of your information.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Your trust is paramount to us. We handle your personal and usage data with the utmost care and transparency, ensuring it is used solely to enhance your experience with Puff Tracker.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: When you register for an account, we collect essential information such as your email address, a chosen nickname, and a securely hashed version of your password. This data is fundamental for creating and managing your personalized user experience.
  • Usage Data: As you interact with the app, we collect data related to your smoking logs. This includes the count of cigarettes, your associated mood, location, and the timestamp of each entry. This data is anonymized where possible and used to provide meaningful insights into your habits.
  • Feedback Data: Should you choose to provide feedback, we collect your contact email, the feedback message itself, and optionally your mobile number. Any provided screenshots are handled with care, strictly for bug reproduction or feature understanding.

How We Utilize Your Information

Your data is exclusively utilized to deliver and continually enhance the Puff Tracker service, ensuring it remains a valuable tool for your journey:

  • To personalize your dashboard, offering tailored insights and progress visualizations.
  • To enable you to effectively track your smoking patterns and set achievable goals.
  • To drive continuous improvement in our app's features and overall user experience.
  • To promptly respond to your feedback, support requests, and address any technical issues.

Data Security Measures

We implement a robust array of security measures designed to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your password is never stored in plain text; it is converted into a secure, irreversible hash. All data is managed through Google Sheets and accessed exclusively via Google Apps Script, employing token-based authentication to secure communication.

Data Sharing Policy

Puff Tracker operates under a strict policy of non-disclosure regarding your personally identifiable information. We do not engage in the selling, trading, or any form of transfer of your data to external parties. This excludes trusted third-party service providers who are integral to our operations (e.g., Google's infrastructure for Sheets and Drive). These parties are contractually bound to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information.

Your Consent & Policy Updates

By utilizing our website and services, you implicitly consent to the terms outlined in this privacy policy. We reserve the right to update or modify this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any revisions will be promptly posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it regularly to stay informed.
