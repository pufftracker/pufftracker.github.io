About Puff Tracker

Welcome to Puff Tracker, your intelligent companion in the journey towards a healthier, smoke-free life. We believe that awareness is the first step towards lasting change.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals to understand and manage their smoking habits through insightful data and personalized tracking. By logging each cigarette, along with your mood and location, Puff Tracker helps you identify your unique triggers, visualize your patterns, and celebrate every step of your progress.

What We Offer

Puff Tracker goes beyond simple counting. We provide a comprehensive suite of tools designed to give you a deeper understanding of your habits:

  • **Detailed Logging:** Record each cigarette with associated mood and location.
  • **Visual Analytics:** See your daily, weekly, and monthly smoking trends with intuitive charts.
  • **Trigger Identification:** Discover what emotional states or environments lead to cravings.
  • **Goal Setting:** Set personalized reduction or smoke-free streak goals to stay motivated.
  • **Progress Tracking:** Celebrate milestones like longest smoke-free streaks and best days.
  • **Data Export:** Easily export your personal data for your records.

Our Philosophy

We believe that true control comes from understanding. Instead of just telling you to quit, Puff Tracker provides the knowledge to empower your choices. Whether you're looking to cut down gradually, quit entirely, or simply gain more mindfulness over your habit, our app is designed to support you every step of the way.
